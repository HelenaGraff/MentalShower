Information Information

In the Vent2Learn app, here you quickly and surely have control over the settings that enable you to create the best experience of the present indoor climate.
Below is an overview of the parameteres that contribute to this activity and the functions that you have access to:
Login: Log in with your GoogleAccount, Facebook, or Sign up with your desired e-mail address and Password.
Home: See real-time notifications of the weather outside and the indoor climate in your classroom environment.
Mood: Change AirSpeed, Temperature, Humidity and AirQuality according to your own perception of warm/cold, humid/dry.
Room view: See the users with similar preferences and join them or turn on the system for yourself only.
Your profile: Store your personal information and keep a journal of your interactions.
